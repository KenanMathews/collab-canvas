Welcome to the Collaborative Canvas!

Welcome to Collaborative Canvas! Here, you can freely express your creativity on a large canvas by drawing in the editor. Your pixel art creations can be posted randomly anywhere on the canvas and utilize our text-to-image generator to instantly create pixel art images. Remember, the canvas resets daily, giving you endless opportunities to fill it with your imagination once again. Happy creating!

To get started:

  1. Click on the "Editor" tab.
  2. Upload an image
  3. Switch to the "Preview" tab to view your image.
